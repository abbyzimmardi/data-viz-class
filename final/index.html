27 states, plus D.C., teach both sex and HIV education

11 states teach just HIV education

7 states teach no sex or HIV education

2 states teach only sex education

The majority of states that have state mandated sex or HIV education, stress the importance of abstinence.





















Sixteen states include negative information about teen pregnancy in their mandated sex and/or HIV education.


Of the 16 states, nine inlcude information on contraception and six do not include any information on contracpetion, only abstinence.

    The six states are:

  • Florida
  • Indiana
  • Mississippi
  • Missouri
  • Ohio
  • Tennessee
